FLIGHT SCHOOL

As my twenties begin to wrap up, I’m starting to ask myself: what do I actually like in life?

Is lamb my favorite meat or did my parents tell me it should be?

Do I actually enjoy all my hobbies or do I just think they look cool?

Are my mannerisms really "being polite" or am I just afraid of rejection?

I don’t want to think about any of these questions on my birthday :)

But in the vein of getting to know myself better this year, there are some MUCH easier questions to ask!

Do I actually like Minor figures oat milk best, or do I just like that they put me on the cover of the bottle?

Is Simply Orange orange juice actually better, or am I just a sheep in Big Orange’s capitalist game?

Is Tito's really the best vodka or do I just have decades-long brand loyalty?

So I’m enrolling in flight school!

All of the tests in flight school are enjoyable little flights where you get to vote for the best and worst versions of that item.

As you vote, each item’s score will be tallied and we’ll figure out the person with the most basic tastes (most matching the group consensus) the biggest contrarian (did you burn your tongue?) and the person with tastes most similar to me (it’s my birthday!)

As per FAA regulations, Flight School is on a more strict schedule than past years’ parties. If you miss the safety demonstration at 8PM we cannot guarantee that you make your first 5 flights of temperature-sensitive foods.

And due to national familial leave policies, the entire curriculum will last less than 2 hours so that enrollees may return home to their pets <3 No Patches, Leia, or Felix will be left behind on my watch.

A boarding pass has been issued in your name, and you may bring one carry-on item of less than 77 inches.
